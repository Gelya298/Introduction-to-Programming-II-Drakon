Title

 Operating systems for Soviet space mission center

Contents

 The project goals were design, implementation and maitenence of the real time operating systems for multicomputer system AS-6 (including BESM-6, CP AS-6, PM AS-6) customised for needs of control of the flying of space ships.

Customer and partners

 CNIIMASH

Time period/duration

 1971-1984

Title

 Local network for Soviet space mission center

Contents

 The goals of the project were design (including the design of the set of protocols), implementation and maitenence of the heterogeneus local area network, customised for needs of control of the flying of space ships. During the project time two variants of the protocol sets were designed and implemented.

Title

 Microkernel object-oriented operating system "CLUSTOS" (a.k.a. "CLOS")

Contents

 The goals of the project were design and implementation of portable object-oriented operating system CLUSTOS, including the following:
  • microkernel,
  • C based cluster programming language (C-clos),
  • CASE for cluster subsystem design and assembling (ProClos),
  • relational DBMS,
  • Binary UNIX emulator

Customer and partners

 ICP AS USSR

Time period/duration

 1985-1993

Title

 Real-time language for on-board computer of space shuttle "BURAN" and software development environment.

Contents

 The language and software development environment was intended for high reliable software production. The static semantics of the languages was specified in Meta-IV (VDM). The specification defined a uniform internal representation of the programs. The represtation integrated all tools constituted the software development environment.
